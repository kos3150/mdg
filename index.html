About

I was born in Pasadena, California. I studied Graphic Information Technology, Graphic Design, and Photography at Arizona State University, where I earned my Bachelor of Science Degree in Graphic Information Technology.

I live with my wife Joy and our two cats Styxx and Blaze, and I work as a freelance graphic designer, web designer, and developer from my home-based graphic design studio in Upland, California.

Values: Teamwork, Excellence, Family, and Respect

Graphic Design, Web Design/Development and Publishing

Audience: Large corporations in the Entertainment industry that require Advertising Marketing and Design Solutions and share the core values of sustainability conservation and transparency.

Positioning: I help large corporations build sustainable systems that help reduce their carbon footprint and conserve energy while making a conscious decision to share their vision.

Purpose: Contribute to efforts to leave the world a better place by adopting a more sustainable and ethical lifestyle.

Promise: Make a measured effort and contribution in support of water conservation, recycling, sustainability, and global warming.

Ambition: Work with five companies in 2025 to create solutions promoting a remote-first work culture.

Proud Navy Veteran

I am a proud Vietnam Era United States Navy Veteran. I received my Honorable Discharge in 1976 at the former Naval Station Treasure Island, located in San Francisco Bay, midway between San Francisco and Oakland, California.

The site consists of two contiguous islands: the northern island named Treasure Island (TI) and the southern island named Yerba Buena or (YBI).
