Advertising/Marketing

Planet of the Apes is a science fiction media franchise consisting of films, books, television series, comics, and other media about a post-apocalyptic world in which humans and intelligent apes clash for control as the dominant species.

The movie poster is one of the best projects in graphic design, and this poster needed to be exceptional in layout and design. The poster design called for emphasis on creating an element of fear, but it also needed to make the viewer want to go to the movie theater and pay for a ticket to watch the movie.

The final “Rise of the Planet of the Apes” movie poster is compelling and draws the viewer's attention to the realistic details in the face of the Ape. It looks so real that it’s almost believable, in that the Ape looks real, and we all know the Ape is not real. It’s just that good, just that imaginative.
